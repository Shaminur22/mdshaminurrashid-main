Network_Scanner

This network scanner discovers all hosts on a network and reports the total number of devices found. It detects vendor/manufacturer information (via MAC/OUI lookups) for each device to help identify hardware sources.Lightweight and easy to use, the tool is ideal for network administrators and students who need a quick inventory and device-identification utility.
